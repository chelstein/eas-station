About Local Authorities

Local authorities (county EMA directors, sheriff's offices, municipal emergency managers) can be granted access to issue EAS alerts for their political subdivision. Each authority is assigned an 8-character station identifier (per the EAS plan), originator code, and restricted to specific FIPS codes and event types. When they use the Broadcast Builder, the system automatically uses their station identity instead of the station default.
