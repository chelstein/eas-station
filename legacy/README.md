### audio/eas_monitor_simple.py
**Archived:** December 2025
**Original Location:** `app_core/audio/eas_monitor_simple.py`

**Description:** A simplified EAS monitor with "no watchdogs, no restarts, no complexity." This was an experimental approach to reduce complexity but was superseded by the production `eas_monitor.py` and the improved `eas_monitor_v2.py` implementations.
